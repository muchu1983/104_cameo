What is Food Love?



Food Love / Alimento Y Amor, a nourishing bi-lingual book, is about to become a vibrant mural in the heart of downtown Santa Cruz. 
Three elegant images, with compelling text in French, English, and Spanish, will enrich and beautify our canvas - the currently blank wall facing the lively Downtown Santa Cruz Farmer's Market.

Led by the author and creator of Food Love/Alimento Y Amor, M.Sophia Santiago, the mural project is brought to you buy a team of passionate, skilled art professionals, painters, students, community art-lovers and supporters! 


Know who else we need on the team?  YOU.

Art Matters!

Another mural downtown? YES! One that includes the community in the making,  illustrating the viability of a creative economy of, and by, the community.

  • During the production of the mural, Food Love/Alimento Y Amor supports artists and shows one pathway to young people considering a career as an artist. Led by muralist and Santa Cruz native Elijah Pfotenhauer, five students from his high school mural program and a Cabrillo College art student will be hired and paid to paint the 60-foot-long mural along with him, a prime example of the Santa Cruz's creative economy in action!


  • Once it's up on the wall, Food Love/Alimento Y Amor reminds those who come in contact with the message, of the farm-to-table journey their food takes, and encourages healthy choices for our community. 

    The     multi-lingual content is inclusive and welcoming to all ages, so the message and beauty will not be lost on even the little ones!
  • While we are thrilled to have received the blessing on the City of Santa Cruz City Council and Arts Commission, funding for Food Love/Alimento Y Amor funding will come from individuals and businesses like yours, giving the community "Ownership" of the project, and the pride of being a tangible part of the team.


What its Going to Take, and What We are Ready to Give.

Let's face it, your financial contributions
are vital to the success of this project!

So, please, contribute money at any level --
because this will be the CooLest Thing you Do all Week!

A financial commitment of $5-500 is just one way to help!
Be part of our team by writing, producing, sharing, posting, liking, painting, showing up, eating, and asking other folks to join in the campaign.

While our team members have each volunteered countless hours in the arts locally, they also earn their living in the arts: teaching, photographing, modeling, administrating, painting - you get the idea.  Our plan for the project includes a real-world budget that supports the artists and arts professionals involved. 

  • If we reach our funding goal, we'll give 70% of it to the artists, students, and professionals that created, administered and produced the piece! (Don't fret! If we don't reach our entire goal, the mural can still proceed, but our creative team will all be donating part of their time. Your contribution will be put to use in the production of this piece, no matter what!)

  • 10% of the $13,300 budget is for paint and other supplies, as well as wall cleaning and preparation costs.

  • 5% goes back to IndieGogo. We couldn't do it without them as part of the team!

  • And the rest pays for the perks!
    (We're working hard to be sure the perks are fabulous, but still keep the budget down. If we exceed our goal, you can bet those gourmet perks will be among the best you've ever tasted!)

What perks, you ask?

  • Snack and Appetizer supporters will get Food Love Buttons and copies of the books in English and Spanish, respectively.


  • How about a gourmet picnic lunch for Brown Bag level donors? You'll come to the mural inaugural launch with other cool people who feel the Food Love - on a Wednesday Farmers' Market day.

  • At the Fine Dining level, you get access to The Curated Feast, complete with custom cocktail and copies of Food Love. 



    What is The Curated Feast?     Liz Birnbaum combines her love of food, art and history as she tells her dinner guests the stories behind their meal. 
  • For those at Banquet level, VIP entry to the Curated Feast is Guaranteed! Complete with maître d's access to the chef's recipes, and a gourmet goody bag courtesy of the Santa Cruz Community Farmers Market.  AND your name or logo goes on the mural.
  • And contributers at the Underwriter level will simply support the mural without reward of perks, because that's what patrons of the Arts do!  But you WILL have your name or company logo featured prominently on the mural dedication and credits.

Can't take us up on our delicious perks, but still want to be on the team? Great!  It's so simple:

  • Share the Food Love Indiegogo Campaign with an art-loving or farmer's-marketing Friend or Network.
  • Blog, post or tweet, #FoodLove #AlimentoYAmor - Let the World know why YOU support Food Love! 
  • Then give yourself a BIG THANK YOU for taking your precious time to watch, to consider, and to CLICK.

Our Team is Ready to Roll

  • Our contract with the owners of the building, Toadal Fitness, is signed!

  • Our support already includes commitments from local businesses (George Ow & William Ow, and New Leaf Community Markets) to support the campaign financially.

  • Our meetings with the Board of Santa Cruz Community Farmers Market continue, and we are excited that they are taking ownership of the piece by contributing and helping us to shape the message on the first panel of the mural.

  • Local, regional, and even national political figures, have expressed their gratitude and support for Food Love! 

A personal goal of M. Sophia Santiago's is to invite First Lady, Michelle Obama, to visit the Farmers Market and see one of the ripple effects of her Lets Move! Program in Action. 


The Visionary Path to Fruition :
This Mural Project is dedicated to Congressman Sam Farr's many contributions to our Community over the decades of his service. Before Sam retires this year wouldn't it be fine to see Sam Farr standing with the First Lady at the Food Love Wall in 2016.  Both Sam Farr and myself have lobbied for the Invitation to Michelle Obama to visit the Central Coast since 2009. Have faith this Campaign will help bring it about!

~M. Sophia Santiago
